Nasi Goreng Kampung

Nasi goreng kampung is one of the fried rice dishes that has been varied by our own community. It has a special feature where we can identify the fried rice of this village by just looking at the vegetables in it. Often, the village fried rice will have water spinach in it and decorated with crackers and cucumber slices to further diversify the taste. Now, with the recipe we have prepared, you can cook this dish yourself.

INGREDIENTS

  • 20g cooking oil
  • 25g small shallots
  • 15g garlic - grinded
  • 15g anchovies
  • 10g bird’s eye chilli - knocked
  • 100g chicken breast - diced

FRIED RICE

  • 300g rice
  • 1 pc chicken egg
  • 80g water spinach
  • 10g seasoning powder

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Stir fry the onion and garlic until fragrant. Then, add the anchovies, bird’s eye chillies and chicken breast.
  2. Add eggs, white rice, water spinach and seasoning powder. Stir well until well combined.
